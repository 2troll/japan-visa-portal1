Japan Study Visa Guide

What is a Japan Student Visa?

A Japan Student Visa allows international students to pursue education at Japanese universities, language schools, and technical colleges. This visa enables you to live in Japan for the duration of your studies and work part-time.

Who Qualifies?

  • University degree students
  • Japanese language school students
  • Vocational school students
  • Research students and scholars
  • Exchange students
