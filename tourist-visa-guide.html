Japan Tourist Visa Guide

Do I Need a Tourist Visa?

Many countries have visa-free agreements with Japan, allowing short-term tourism without a visa. Check your country's specific requirements below.

Visa-Free Entry (68 countries)

  • Stay up to 90 days for tourism
  • No visa application required
  • Valid passport required
  • Return/onward ticket required

Who Needs a Visa?

  • Citizens from countries without visa waiver agreements
  • Those staying longer than visa-free period
  • Visitors with specific purposes (business, family visits)
