Japan Residency & Permanent Visa Guide

Pathways to Permanent Residency in Japan

Permanent residency allows you to live in Japan indefinitely without visa renewals. Several pathways exist depending on your circumstances.

Types of Long-Term Visas

  • Permanent Resident - Indefinite stay, no work restrictions
  • Spouse/Child of Japanese National - Family-based residence
  • Long-Term Resident - Special circumstances
  • Investor/Business Manager - Business establishment

Requirements Overview

  • 10 years of continuous residence (work visa holders)
  • Good conduct (no criminal record)
  • Financial stability (¥3,000,000+ annual income)
  • Tax compliance (paid all taxes)
  • Japanese language ability (N2 recommended)
