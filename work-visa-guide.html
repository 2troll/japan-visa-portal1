Japan Work Visa Guide

What is a Japan Work Visa?

A Japan Work Visa allows foreign nationals to live and work in Japan legally. The most common type is the Engineer/Specialist in Humanities/International Services visa, which covers a wide range of professional occupations.

Who Qualifies?

  • IT professionals and engineers
  • English teachers and educators
  • Business and marketing professionals
  • Healthcare workers
  • Translators and interpreters
  • Designers and creative professionals
